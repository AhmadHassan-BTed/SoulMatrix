## **Why Relationships Move in Cycles** 

We are used to thinking love is a straight line. That if people are truly made for each other, everything should be smooth and easy. And when a difficult period comes — we panic. We think: that’s it, love is over, we made a mistake, this was the wrong person. 

This is a profound delusion — and it has destroyed more couples than any betrayal. Relationships are alive. They have breath — an inhale and an exhale. There are high tides, when you’re carried toward each other, and low tides, when you want to step aside. There are years when the union surges upward, builds, expands — and years when it seems to freeze, goes inward, is tested for strength. 

This is not a malfunction. It is a rhythm. Each couple has its own, and it’s set not by mood and not by fatigue — but by the energy of time, which changes from year to year by precise laws. The problem is that most people live through these dips blindly. They don’t understand why it suddenly became hard. Why they drifted apart. Why what worked before — stopped. And they take a natural dip for the end of feeling. They leave at the darkest moment — not knowing that a rise always follows it. 

But the one who knows the rhythm — doesn’t panic. They understand: this is low tide, and that’s normal. Soon there will be high tide. And instead of destroying — they protect. 

## **I Don’t Believe in Coincidences** 

Not in love, not in breakups, not in why one year gives a couple closeness and another — trials. Behind all of it stands a precise mechanics. The energy of time. 

Every year you and your partner enter a new energy. It sets the tone for everything — the mood, the events, what you’ll go through together. One year is made for building and strengthening. Another — for rethinking. A third — for surviving a crisis and emerging stronger. 

This energy doesn’t ask your permission. It comes on its own — by the laws of the Matrix of Destiny, by a rhythm that was set before you even met. You may not know about it — but you live through it all the same. The only question is: blindly or consciously.

## **How to Read a Couple’s Forecast** 

We take the triple key of each partner and add them together. We get the shared energy of the couple for this period. 

For example, Nika’s key is 17-11-10, and Hope’s key is 4-15-19. The shared key will look like this: 1. 17 + 4 = 21 

2. 11 + 15 = 26; 2 + 6 = 8 3. 10 + 19 = 29; 2 + 9 = 11 

The couple’s program: 21 — 8 — 11 

Here it’s important to keep one thing in mind: everyone’s birthday falls at a different time. This means one of the partners may have already entered a new energy — while the other still remains in the previous one. There’s nothing wrong with this. We simply take each person’s current indicators at the present moment and add them. 

A more detailed calculation of short periods — quarterly and monthly — is covered in the compatibility course. There you’ll learn to work with the subtlest fluctuations in a relationship. 

## **How to Read the Forecast** 

First we look at the individual energies of each partner — separately. What is he going through? What is she going through? What program is each one passing through right now? Then we look at the shared energy of the couple. This is the field in which your union exists during this period. 

Each energy has its own interpretation. The pluses show where the union can strengthen and where opportunities open. The minuses — where awareness is needed and where the risks lie. And always — recommendations on how to bring everything into the plus. 

Forecasting is a code. Knowing it, you stop being passengers in your own relationship. You become the ones who steer.

Marriage and making it official — arcana 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 17, 19, 20. These energies favor a relationship moving to the official level — engagement, wedding, the registry office, shared housing, a firm decision about the future. 

Prosperity, career, and money — arcana 3, 4, 7, 11, 13, 15, 16, 19. These periods are favorable for financial breakthroughs, joint business, major investments, and reaching a new material level. Relocations and changes of place — arcana 7, 10, 12, 16, 21, 22. These energies often bring a change of space into the couple’s life — a relocation, a trip that changes everything, a new country, a new home. 

Difficult periods requiring awareness — arcana 8, 9, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 18, 20. These energies carry trials and tests. They are not bad — they are transformative. But they must be passed through carefully and consciously, otherwise the minuses won’t keep you waiting. Remember: no arcana is a sentence. Even the most difficult period, lived through consciously, raises a couple to a new level. And the most favorable one — through carelessness — can be missed. 

## **Arcana 1. The Magician. The Pioneer.** 

_Only in the personal forecast — in the year of birth and at ages 20, 40, 60 (at 40 and 60 as the second energy)._ 

This is one of the most powerful periods in a person’s life. Everything you conceive has a chance to take form. Ideas find shape, plans come to life, doors open where before there was a dead end. This is a time of active movement forward. New knowledge, new contacts, new projects. The mind is literally tuned to absorb information — learn, soak it up, grow. Promotions, business launches, creative breakthroughs are all possible. 

You are seen. You are heard. In this period you are able to inspire others — simply by your presence and your word. 

### **What to watch for** 

The main trap of this period is overconfidence. A sense of superiority can turn into conflicts and a loss of authority. Watch your documents — mistakes and misunderstandings are possible. Control your thoughts — in this period they materialize with doubled force. 

### **Recommendations** 

Set goals and write them down. Seek collaboration with professionals. Strengthen the body — especially the spine and the lower part. Practice meditation. Visit places of power. And work on your self-worth — this period manifests exactly as much as you believe in yourself. 

## **Arcana 2. The High Priestess. Unity and Harmony.** 

### _In the personal forecast — in the year of birth, and at ages 10, 20, 40, and 60. In a couple’s forecast it appears extremely rarely._ 

This is a period of silence and depth. A time when there’s no need to storm and conquer — there’s a need to listen. Listen to yourself, listen to life, trust what is happening. 

Intuition sharpens to its limit in this period. Information that was long hidden may open to you. Secrets surface, the concealed comes to light. Don’t ask — just observe. The answers will come on their own. 

This is a soft, spiritual period. A good time for learning, gathering information, developing inner abilities. Relationships with the mother and the women of the lineage come to the foreground — it’s important to harmonize them. Joyful events connected with children are possible. 

### **What to watch for** 

The main trap is trying to control everything with logic. It doesn’t work here. Also dangerous are intrigues, gossip, participation in others’ games. A third party may appear in the relationship — a woman who brings instability. Watch the information you pass on and receive. 

### **Recommendations** 

Trust your intuition — in this period it is your main instrument. Record important promises in writing. Practice yoga, meditation, pranayama. Create a harmonious space around you. Watch your nutrition — more natural and plant-based. And remember — the world is not divided into black and white.
